Our Goals

With your donations, we aim to achieve the following goals to support people with disabilities:

  • Increase access to adaptive medical equipment.
  • Improve the quality of available specialized care.
  • Provide support programs for families and caregivers.
  • Promote the inclusion of people with disabilities in society.
